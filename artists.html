Questions?

Is this for professionals only?

No. Atelier is for all serious artists—professionals, amateurs, students. If you care about your craft, you belong here.

How do I find collaborators?

Create a profile, describe your discipline and what you're looking for. Browse artists, search by specialty and location, message directly.

What does it cost to book spaces?

Pricing varies by space. Founding artists get 20% off commissions for life.

Can I influence the platform?

Yes. Founding artists shape the product roadmap. Your feedback directly influences features and policies.
