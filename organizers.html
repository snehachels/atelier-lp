Questions About Organizing on Atelier?

What can I list on Atelier?

Rehearsal studios, practice rooms, performance spaces, dance halls, multi-purpose studios, lofts with mirrors. Anything dancers use. You set the rate and availability.

How much does it cost to list?

Listing your space is free. When a booking happens, Atelier takes a commission from the rental. Founding hosts get special commission rates locked in for life.

How do I set my rates?

You're in control. Set your hourly rate, half-day and full-day rates, and any special pricing. You can change rates anytime based on demand and availability.

How do I get paid?

Payments are processed through Atelier within 24 hours of a rental. Money goes directly to your bank account. No waiting, no checks.

What if I have a cancellation?

You set your cancellation policy. Dancers are warned before booking. Atelier protects both hosts and renters with clear, transparent policies.

Can I influence the platform?

Absolutely. Founding hosts shape the product roadmap. Your feedback directly influences features, policies, and how we serve the community. We build with you, not for you.

What about insurance and liability?

You maintain your own space insurance as always. Atelier hosts liability information clearly to renters. We recommend everyone carrying appropriate coverage.
