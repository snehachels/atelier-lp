Questions?

How does DanceHub work?

Create a profile, find and connect with local dancers in your style. Browse rehearsal spaces and book them. Discover and teach classes. Find and attend performances. It's like having a home for your entire dance life—all in one place.

How much does it cost?

Browsing and connecting with dancers is FREE. If you book a space, we take a small commission from the host. If you teach classes, there's a listing fee. Early members get special pricing and lifetime perks.

Is this just for professionals or also for amateurs?

Both! Beginners, hobbyists, and professionals. Whether you dance for fun or it's your career, there's a place for you here. In fact, the mix is what makes the community valuable.

How is this different from Instagram or Facebook Groups?

Instagram is scattered. Facebook Groups are chaotic. We're building a real platform designed specifically for dancers—with profiles, collaboration matching, space booking, class discovery, and event management all integrated. Think of us as the "home base" for your dance community.

What about space liability and safety?

Great question. We'll have verification features, reviews, and clear safety guidelines. We recommend renters and hosts use their own insurance (like most peer-to-peer platforms). We're transparent about this, not hiding it.

When does it launch?

We're building the MVP now and will launch with a small community in [Your City] in [Month]. Early members get 3 months free access and special lifetime perks. Join now to secure your spot.

My city isn't listed. Can you launch there?

We're focusing on one city first to build a strong community. If you're interested in your city being next, join the waitlist and let us know—we'll keep you in the loop.
