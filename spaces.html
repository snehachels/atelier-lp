Questions About Hosting?

What can I list on Atelier?

Rehearsal studios, practice rooms, performance spaces, recording studios, multi-purpose studios, artist lofts, and more. Any space where artists create, collaborate, or perform. You set the rate and availability.

How much does it cost to list?

Listing your space is free. When a booking happens, Atelier takes a commission from the rental. Founding space owners get special commission rates locked in for life.

How do I set my rates?

You're in control. Set your hourly rate, half-day and full-day rates, and any special pricing. You can change rates anytime based on demand and availability.

How do I get paid?

Payments are processed through Atelier within 24 hours of a rental. Money goes directly to your bank account. No waiting, no checks.

What if I have a cancellation?

You set your cancellation policy. Artists are warned before booking. Atelier protects both space owners and renters with clear, transparent policies.

Can I influence the platform?

Absolutely. Founding space owners shape the product roadmap. Your feedback directly influences features, policies, and how we serve the community. We build with you, not for you.

What about insurance and liability?

You maintain your own space insurance as always. Atelier displays liability information clearly to renters. We recommend everyone carrying appropriate coverage.
