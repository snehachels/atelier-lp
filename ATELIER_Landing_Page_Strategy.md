# ATELIER: Landing Page Strategy

## Primary Objective
Capture emails from serious dancers and build founding member list before product launch. Establish Atelier as a credible, artist-focused community platform (not another transactional marketplace).

---

## Target Audience (Phase 1)
- **Professional dancers** (choreographers, teachers, performers)
- **Serious amateurs** (practice 4+ days/week, care about craft)
- **Location:** [City] first, then expanding
- **Mindset:** "I need real collaborators and a real home for my art"

---

## Key Landing Page Workflows

### Workflow 1: First-Time Visitor
1. Land on page → See compelling hero + tagline
2. Scroll through features → Understand value proposition
3. See social proof or "built by dancers" messaging → Build trust
4. Reach CTA section → Enter email
5. Confirm signup → Get confirmation

**Success:** Email captured, visitor feels "this gets me"

### Workflow 2: Skeptical Visitor
1. Land → See "Not a Marketplace. A Home." headline
2. Read "Built by Dancers, For Dancers" section → Validation
3. Review "Gap We're Solving" → Understand the problem
4. Check FAQ → Answer lingering questions
5. Either: Convert to email OR leave (acceptable)

**Success:** Reduced skepticism, increases conversion

### Workflow 3: Return Visitor
1. Come back to page (from social/email)
2. Quickly scan to confirm → Already interested
3. Sign up with email

**Success:** Email captured on second exposure

## Content Hierarchy (What to Lead With)

### Hero Section (Most Important)
- **Goal:** Stop visitor in 3 seconds
- **Message:** "Where Dancers Create Together"
- **Why it works:** Speaks directly to the pain (isolation) + solution (community)

### Features Section
- **Goal:** Show immediate value
- **Message:** "Everything You Need" — collaborators, spaces, teaching, performing
- **Format:** 4 concrete, relatable features (not generic "easy" or "fast")

### Why Atelier Section
- **Goal:** Build credibility + show listening
- **Message:** "We talked to 50+ dancers. Here's what they said."
- **Format:** Validate the actual problems dancers face

### The Gap Section
- **Goal:** Differentiate from competitors
- **Message:** Show fragmentation vs. integration clearly
- **Format:** Side-by-side before/after

### CTA Section
- **Goal:** Convert
- **Message:** "Be a Founding Member" (exclusive, early, special)
- **Incentive:** Early access + lifetime perks

### FAQ Section
- **Goal:** Answer objections
- **Message:** Transparency + honesty about liability, cost, timeline
- **Benefit:** Reduce friction for fence-sitters

## Copy Principles (Non-Negotiable)

✅ **DO:**
- Lead with story ("We talked to dancers...")
- Use insider language ("choreography," "rehearsal," "performance")
- Validate before selling
- Be specific (not "easy 3-click booking" — specific problems)
- Show understanding ("Stop wasting hours coordinating through DMs")

❌ **DON'T:**
- Use corporate startup language ("disrupt," "move fast," "join the movement")
- Make generic promises ("find your tribe")
- Use trendy language (changes too fast)
- Hide fees or timeline
- Sound like a transaction ("marketplace," "platform," "app")

---

## Design Principles (Non-Negotiable)

✅ **DO:**
- Lots of whitespace (creative studio feel)
- Warm, earthy colors (Rust, Sage, Warm Dark, Cream)
- Montserrat + Crimson Text (modern + editorial)
- Real dancer photos (candid, in motion, diverse)
- Readable on all devices (mobile-first)

❌ **DON'T:**
- Busy layouts or animations
- Stock photos or generic "happy people"
- Cold tech colors (grays, blues, blacks)
- Tiny fonts or cramped spacing
- Anything that screams "tech startup"

## Launch Workflow

1. **Finalize page** (copy + images + email integration) → This week
2. **Deploy to Vercel** (git push → auto-deploy) → Day 1
3. **Point custom domain** (atelierdance.com to Vercel) → Day 1
4. **Test thoroughly** (mobile, desktop, email signup) → Day 2
5. **Share on social** (Instagram, TikTok, Twitter) → Day 3
6. **Share with dance community** (DMs, emails, studios) → Day 3+
7. **Monitor metrics** (visits, signups, feedback) → Ongoing
8. **Interview signers** (call 5-10 early members) → Week 2+
